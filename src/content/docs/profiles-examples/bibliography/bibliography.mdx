OLS content packages SHOULD include their own exact citations. The following are recommended categories and commonly useful resources for building EOTC and Oriental Orthodox teaching material.

        
| Source | Use |
| --- | --- |
| *Mäṣḥafä Qeddase* | Primary source for the Divine Liturgy and anaphoras. |
| *Deggwa* | Saint Yared’s hymnody and chant tradition. |
| *Mäṣḥafä Sä‘atat* | Book of Hours and prayer structure. |
| *Synaxarium of the Ethiopian Church* | Saints, commemorations, and feast teaching. |
